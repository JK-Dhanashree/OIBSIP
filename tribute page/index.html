1981 - Present

Biography

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, also known as MS Dhoni, is an Indian cricketer and former captain of the Indian national team. Under his captaincy, India won numerous prestigious tournaments, including the 2007 ICC T20 World Cup, the 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup, and the 2013 ICC Champions Trophy.

Dhoni is regarded as one of the greatest wicket-keepers and captains in cricket history, known for his calm demeanor and sharp decision-making under pressure. His leadership style transformed the Indian team, leading to a new era in Indian cricket.
